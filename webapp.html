Help Section
The basic idea for Tic-Tac-Tie-Too-To is that it's a three-dimensional Tic-Tac-To game with a length of 4 needed to win in any direction.
Each of the sixteen boxes represents a row running depthwise into the screen. With the topleft sqare of each box representing the foremost block, moving clockwise in the box corresponds to moving deeper into the screen. This makes the bottom left block the deepest.
